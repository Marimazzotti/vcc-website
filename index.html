The Kabaret of Broken Dolls

In the dim glow of a Prague cabaret, ten women take the stage — polished, precise, and perfectly controlled.
They are dolls. But beneath the surface, something begins to shift. As music unravels the rules that once held them together, cracks form.
What starts as performance becomes awakening — each woman confronting who she is beyond the roles she has been given.
Raw, playful, and unapologetically feminine — a story of women who don't destroy the system, but learn to step beyond it. Together.

— The Company

We Are the Velvet Curtain Collective

Velvet Curtain Collective was born in Prague, built by women who arrived from different corners of the world and found each other through a love for music and theater.
We are singers, musicians, dancers, performers, creators, storytellers, and collaborators who believe the stage is a place for creative freedom.
Together, we do musical theater that celebrates who we are, our skills, and where we come from.

Musical Director

Amy

Co-founder of VCC and the International Women of Prague Choir. Michigan State music graduate. Has performed globally — including twice at the White House — and directed over 20 professional musical theatre productions in the US. Arranged the full show repertoire and wrote three original songs for the Broken Dolls show.

Artistic Director

Lucila

Co-founder of VCC. Singer, actress, and producer with over ten years in musical theatre. Credits include Next to Normal, Cabaret, Aida, Les Misérables, and Moulin Rouge — the latter two also co-directed and co-produced. Trained at the Lee Strasberg Academy in New York and an Estill Voice Trainee Level 2.

Performer

Danielle

Powerful voice and wide range, rooted in regional, state, and all-state choirs and school musicals. Behind the scenes, she keeps the Collective running — managing schedules and logistics, to make our dreams become reality. Motivated, organised, and relentlessly get-things-done.

Performer

Laura

Natural singer and interpreter with vocal training and a developing stage presence. Experience in commercial acting, modelling, and content creation, with a focus on creating authentic and engaging work. Also involved in managing the Collective’s social media presence.

Performer

Marina

Versatile singer, strongly grounded in music theory. Multi-instrument player, and trained hip hop/modern dancer. Has arranged songs for vocal ensembles and loves singing in harmony. Supports the Collective with PR and web design, choreographed one song for the Broken Dolls show.

Performer

Gabby

Holds a degree in Music with a specialism in Bel Canto, with opera credits including Le Nozze di Figaro and Pagliacci. Moves fluidly between opera and pop, blending both worlds with ease. Contributed to the makeup design concept for Broken Dolls and coached the team on makeup application techniques.

Performer

Teresa

Professional actress, singer, songwriter, and voice artist with over 40 years in the entertainment industry across the US and internationally. Credits span commercials, television, film, theatre, and recording studios. With a heart for musical theatre, she brings a lifelong passion to the Collective.

Performer

CeeCee

Actress and performer based in Prague, with a background in musical theatre and a regular spot at the monthly improv show Script for Brains. Brings electric stage presence and a sharp instinct for both comedy and drama. Part of the Collective's marketing team.

Performer

Stefany

Passionate about theatre and movement, with a background in improvisation and years of experience in swing, tap, and African dance and rhythms. New to musical performance but brings a playful curiosity and genuine enthusiasm to everything she takes on.

Performer

Solomia

Live singer rooted in Prague's Irish pubs and streets, bringing warmth and the sweetest voice in the room to every stage she steps on. Trained violinist of eight years, with a natural instinct for performance and a deep love for musical theatre that led her straight to the Collective.
