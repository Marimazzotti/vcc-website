Cabaret of Broken Dolls

Set in the dim glow of a Prague cabaret, ten women take the stage. Polished dolls with hidden cracks, their stories unfolding through music, movement, and confession. Part cabaret reverie, part emotional reckoning, where illusion slips, vulnerability surfaces, and the broken begin to transform. Raw, playful, and unapologetically feminine, a celebration of rising, together.

— The Company

We Are the Velvet Curtain Collective

Velvet Curtain Collective was born in Prague, built by women who arrived from every corner of the world and found each other through music and theater. We are performers, storytellers, and collaborators who believe the stage is a place for truth — raw, beautiful, and alive. Together, we create musical theater that celebrates where we come from, who we are, and the extraordinary power of women's voices in harmony.
